    Task Manager

    Most of people are overwhelm everyday with tasks. So, they need an app that allows them to write down notes about important tasks that need to be performed. The task manager app will help people to get things done. The goal is to be able to complete the daily to-do list and manage all the important tasks. This a personal project that I choose to strengthen my skills in mobile application development.

    Goal

    Manage ToDo list App.

    About the App

    Regardless of the purpose of the tasks. The TaskManager app will help you get things done in an organized way, this app can be used for assignments (personal or professional), homework, hobbies, events and more. This app helps you collect, retain, organize, and remember all your upcoming tasks .

    The research

    During my past interviews I have been asked about the way I managed my daily task. Whenever I start a new job, I looked for tools to make my daily task organized in order to establish priorities and deadlines, and follow up on advancement to ensure delivering my tasks on time. I’ve been looking for a tool that will be useful for my daily personal and professional tasks, then I came up with the idea of building a mobile app.

    Requirements

    • Manaing Task (CRUD)
    • Organizing Tasks
    • Giving Priority
    • Deadlines and reminders
    • Easy to use and good design
    • Task Domain (personal and professional)
    • Tasks Category
    • Memorized Tasks

    User research

    I used this approach to collect and analyze information received from real users. I started a user survey process to validate my assumptions.

    • Do you write down your to do list?
    • What do you think of managing tasks?
    • What do you use to manage tasks, an App or papers ?
    • What app do you use for To Do list and why?
    • What do you think about a To do App? What do you like to have as features?
    • How do you react when you get things done?
    • How do you react when you forget to do something?

    Visual Design

    Clean Architecture

    The goal of a clean architecture is separation of concerns. This separation is achieved by splitting the app into layers. Each has at least one layer for business rules and one for interfaces. This architectural style creates apps that are easy to test, maintain, and evolve.
